## Course Overview

Nahjul Balagha (The Peak of Eloquence) is a treasure of Islamic wisdom, containing the sermons, letters, and maxims of Imam Ali (AS). This in-depth course takes you through selected passages, exploring themes of spirituality, justice, governance, and ethics that remain profoundly relevant today.

### What You'll Learn

- **Major Sermons (Khutbas)**
  - Khutbatul Shaqshaqiyya (The Sermon of Disparagement)
  - Khutbatul Qasiah (The Sermon without Names)
  - Khutbat al-Muttaqin (Description of the Pious)
  - Khutbat al-Hammam (On the Creation)
  - Sermons on death, resurrection, and divine justice

- **Selected Letters**
  - Letter to Malik al-Ashtar (governance principles)
  - Letter to Uthman ibn Hunayf (on asceticism)
  - Letter to Imam Hasan (AS)
  - Administrative and political correspondence
  - Personal spiritual guidance

- **Wise Sayings (Hikam)**
  - On knowledge and ignorance
  - On patience and gratitude
  - On friendship and enmity
  - On wealth and poverty
  - On this world and the Hereafter

- **Major Themes**
  - Divine unity (Tawhid) and attributes
  - Justice and social equity
  - Leadership and governance
  - Spiritual development
  - Ethics and character building

### Course Structure

- **Weeks 1-8:** Selected Major Sermons
- **Weeks 9-16:** Important Letters and Their Context
- **Weeks 17-22:** Collections of Wise Sayings by Theme
- **Weeks 23-24:** Synthesis and Contemporary Application

### Learning Outcomes

Students will:
- Understand the historical context of Nahjul Balagha
- Appreciate the literary and rhetorical excellence of Imam Ali (AS)
- Extract practical wisdom for modern challenges
- Develop deeper spiritual insights
- Connect Nahjul Balagha to other Islamic sciences

### Who Should Enroll?

- Students seeking spiritual and intellectual growth
- Those interested in Islamic political philosophy
- Individuals wanting to understand Imam Ali's (AS) wisdom
- Anyone looking for guidance on ethics and governance
