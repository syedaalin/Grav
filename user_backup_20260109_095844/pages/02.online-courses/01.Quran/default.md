## Course Overview

Our comprehensive Quran course is designed to help students of all levels develop a profound connection with the Holy Quran. Whether you're starting your journey or looking to perfect your recitation, our qualified instructors provide personalized guidance in Tajweed, memorization, and understanding.

### What You'll Learn

- **Quranic Arabic Fundamentals**
  - Arabic alphabet and pronunciation
  - Proper articulation of letters (Makharij)
  - Common Quranic vocabulary

- **Tajweed Rules**
  - Rules of Noon Sakinah and Tanween
  - Rules of Meem Sakinah
  - Qalqalah, Madd, and other essential rules
  - Practical application in recitation

- **Memorization Techniques**
  - Proven Hifz methodology
  - Retention and review strategies
  - Personalized memorization plans
  - Weekly progress tracking

- **Tafsir Basics**
  - Understanding context and themes
  - Introduction to classical commentaries
  - Practical life applications
